3D Runner

3D Runner is a thrilling survival-based obstacle runner built in Roblox Studio. The player dashes through a dynamic lava-filled environment, dodging explosive traps, collecting coins, and unlocking checkpoints with each successful run. The game features energy boosts, particle effects, and fast-paced music for a truly engaging experience.
